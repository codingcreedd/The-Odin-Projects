Chicken Alfredo

Description

Indulge in the rich and creamy goodness of Homemade Chicken Alfredo Pasta, where tender chicken meets silky fettuccine coated in a luscious garlic Parmesan cream sauce. This classic Italian-American dish is a comforting and satisfying choice for a decadent dinner.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the Fettuccine:
    Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season and Cook the Chicken:
    Season the chicken slices with salt and black pepper. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken until it's no longer pink in the center and has a golden-brown exterior. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Creamy Alfredo Sauce:
    In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and saut√© for a minute until fragrant. Reduce the heat to medium-low. Add the butter, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese. Stir continuously until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Combine Chicken and Pasta:
    Add the cooked chicken slices to the Alfredo sauce, stirring gently to coat the chicken in the creamy goodness. Let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve:
    Pour the chicken Alfredo sauce over the cooked fettuccine pasta. Toss everything together until the pasta is well-coated in the sauce. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  6. Garnish and Enjoy:
    Serve the Chicken Alfredo Pasta hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh parsley. Pair it with a side of garlic bread or a crisp salad for a complete and satisfying meal.
