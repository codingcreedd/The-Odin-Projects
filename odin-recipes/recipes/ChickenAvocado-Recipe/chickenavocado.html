Chicken Avocado

Description

Elevate your salad game with this refreshing and satisfying Grilled Chicken and Avocado Salad. Packed with lean protein, creamy avocado, and a zesty lime dressing, this dish is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients that come together for a delightful culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 2 ripe avocados, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • Zesty Lime Dressing:
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken:
    Season the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, and smoked paprika. Heat olive oil in a grill pan or on an outdoor grill. Cook the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until fully cooked. Allow it to rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.
  2. Assemble the Salad:
    In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, sliced avocados, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and chopped cilantro. If using, sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top.
  3. Prepare the Zesty Lime Dressing:
    In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, minced garlic, honey, salt, and pepper. Adjust the seasoning to your taste.
  4. Combine and Serve:
    Drizzle the zesty lime dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together. Arrange the sliced grilled chicken on top of the salad.
  5. Garnish and Serve:
    Garnish the salad with additional cilantro and a wedge of lime. Serve immediately, savoring the combination of grilled chicken, creamy avocado, and the bright citrusy dressing.
